How to Use

1. Enter your URL in the input field (make sure to include http:// or https://)

2. Click the "Generate QR Code" button or press Enter

3. Your QR code will appear instantly

4. Click "Download QR Code" to save it to your device

5. Access your previous QR codes in the History section
